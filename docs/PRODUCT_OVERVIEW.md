## What Is Mortgage Control Tower?

**Mortgage Control Tower (MCT)** is an AI-powered command center that gives mortgage managers **real-time visibility** into every loan in their pipeline — without digging through spreadsheets or LOS reports.

### Headline Value Proposition

> **Catch Every Risk. Close More Loans.**

MCT doesn't replace your LOS or CRM. It sits on top, giving you the visibility those systems don't provide. Purpose-built for **operations managers**, not loan officers.

### Trust Signals
- ✅ LOS Integration (Encompass, Byte, LendingPad)
- ✅ CRM Connected
- ✅ AI-Powered (OpenAI, Landing AI, Google AI)
- ✅ Trusted by Mortgage Lenders & Brokers
